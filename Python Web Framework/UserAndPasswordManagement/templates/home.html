You don’t have to eat less, you just have to eat right.

Eating less is not going to make you healthy. It sure can make you skinny fat, which is not healthy either. Eat. But choose nutritious foods and practice portion control.

Health is a relationship between you and your body.

The middleman is food. Depending on the type of food you consume, your health will either improve or deteriorate.

Moderation. Small helpings. Sample a little bit of everything. These are the secrets of happiness and good health.

Eating healthy doesn’t mean you have to deprive yourself of the various, delicious food options. There’s no harm in tasting a little bit of everything. Once again, portion control is the key.
